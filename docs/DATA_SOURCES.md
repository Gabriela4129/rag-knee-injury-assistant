# Data Sources

This RAG system uses 11 clinically relevant knee-injury documents (PDFs) stored locally under `data/raw/`.  
Together, they cover ACL injuries, meniscus tears, OA management, return-to-sport protocols, and surgical vs conservative treatment.

### 1. **AAOS Clinical Practice Guideline – Management of ACL Injuries (2022).pdf**
Authoritative AAOS guideline providing evidence-based recommendations for ACL diagnosis, imaging, treatment, and post-operative care.

### 2. **ACL Diagnosis_Physical Test.pdf**
A clinical guide focused on ACL physical examination tests such as Lachman, pivot-shift, and anterior drawer.

### 3. **ACL Reconstruction Post-Op Protocol.pdf**
Detailed rehabilitation protocol following ACL reconstruction, including early-, mid-, and late-phase rehab milestones.

### 4. **ACL_Injury1.pdf**
General overview of ACL injury mechanisms, symptoms, diagnosis, and treatment options (surgical & non-surgical).

### 5. **acl-curbside-consult-return-sports-2.pdf**
A clinician-focused practical guide on return-to-sport recommendations after ACL injury or surgery.

### 6. **acl-return-to-sport-2.pdf**
Formal return-to-sport protocol including functional tests, timelines, and performance criteria.

### 7. **ConservativevsSurgicalTreatmentofAnteriorCruciateLigamentRupture-11.pdf**
Comparison of conservative vs surgical ACL treatment, outcomes, and decision-making criteria.

### 8. **ESSKA – Meniscus Consensus Project (Meniscus Tears).pdf**
ESSKA multidisciplinary consensus on classification, diagnosis, and management of meniscus tears.

### 9. **ESSKA – Meniscus Rehabilitation Consensus (2024 EU–US).pdf**
ESSKA/AOSSM/AASPT formal consensus on meniscus injury rehabilitation and return-to-sport guidelines.

### 10. **Knee Osteoarthritis Evidence Based Guidelines Management.pdf**
Evidence-based guideline for conservative management of knee osteoarthritis.

### 11. **Meniscus_Tear_Pathology_Incidence_and_Management.pdf**
Comprehensive scientific review of meniscus tear pathology, epidemiology, diagnosis, and treatment approaches.

These documents constitute the full knowledge base used for embedding, chunking, and retrieval in the RAG system.
